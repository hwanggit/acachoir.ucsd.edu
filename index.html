FAQ

  • Can anyone join?

    Yes, everyone is welcome to join!


  • Do I have to register?

    Nope! Just come stop by! Looking forward to meet you!


  • What kind of songs does the group sing?

    We mostly sing pop songs. If you have any recommedations, let us know!


  • I don't have any singing experience nor knowing how to read music! Can I still join?

    There are no auditions required. Come sing and have fun!
    We offer individual feedback, just let one of the board members know!


  • I missed the first couple of rehearsals! Can I join halfway in the quarter?

    Yes! We'll catch you up on everything!


  • Will we get to perform?

    If you are interested in performing it will take a little more time
    commitment, just let us know! However this is optional.


  • How is the time commitment like?

    This is a low commitment club.
    You are welcome to come whenever you are free.


  • Do you have a Facebook page?

    Yes! In fact, we mostly update our informations on there.
    Contact us below!
